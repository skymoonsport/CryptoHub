⚙️ Settings & Customization Guide

CryptoHub is fully customizable. Learn how to personalize your experience — from language and theme to filters, sorting, and advanced features.

🌐 Language Settings

CryptoHub supports over 30 languages. The entire interface — including all labels, descriptions, and tooltips — changes instantly when you switch languages.

1

Open the Language Menu

Click the flag icon or language code (e.g., "EN", "AR") in the top-right navigation bar to open the language dropdown.

2

Select Your Language

Scroll through the list and click your preferred language. The page updates immediately — no reload needed.

3

Preference is Saved

Your language choice is saved in your browser's local storage and will be remembered on your next visit.

💡 Arabic and other RTL languages automatically switch the entire layout to right-to-left direction.

🌙 Dark / Light Mode

Switch between dark and light themes to match your preference or environment.

1

Find the Theme Toggle

Look for the sun ☀️ or moon 🌙 icon in the top navigation bar, next to the language selector.

2

Click to Toggle

Click the icon to instantly switch between dark mode (recommended for night use) and light mode (recommended for bright environments).

💡 Your theme preference is saved automatically and persists across sessions.

🔍 Search & Filter Cryptocurrencies

CryptoHub tracks 5,000+ cryptocurrencies. Use search and filters to find exactly what you need.

1

Global Search

Press Ctrl+K (or ⌘K on Mac) to open the search bar. Type any coin name or symbol to find it instantly.

2

Filter by Network

Use the Network dropdown to filter coins by blockchain: Ethereum, BNB Chain, Solana, Polygon, Arbitrum, Bitcoin, and 20+ more networks.

3

Sort the Table

Click any column header to sort by Price, Market Cap, Volume, or 24h Change. Click again to reverse the sort order.

4

Adjust Rows Per Page

Use the "Show" dropdown to display 25, 50, or 100 coins per page. Use the Previous / Next buttons to navigate pages.
