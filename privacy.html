Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

CryptoHub ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Automatically Collected Data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other usage statistics through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to analyze trends, administer the site, and gather demographic information. Third-party services like Google AdSense and Google Analytics may also set cookies.
  • User Preferences: Language selection, theme preference (light/dark), stored locally in your browser.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our cryptocurrency tracking service
  • To improve, personalize, and optimize user experience
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or abuse

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

4. Analytics

We may use analytics services such as Google Analytics to monitor and analyze web traffic. These services may collect data through cookies. You can learn more about Google's practices at Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information with third parties for analytics and advertising purposes.

6. Data Sources & APIs

CryptoHub displays cryptocurrency data from public and premium global APIs. We do not store personal trading data. All market data is provided "as is" without warranty.

7. Cookies Management

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the website. We provide a cookie consent banner upon first visit allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact us via Telegram at @cryptoHubcrypto1 for any privacy-related requests.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
