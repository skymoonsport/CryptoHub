🚀 How to Get Started in 3 Steps

1

Open CryptoHub

Visit cryptohubcoin.com in any browser on desktop, tablet, or mobile. The platform loads instantly — no installation required.

2

Choose Your Language & Theme

Click the language flag in the top navigation to switch to your language. Click the sun/moon icon to toggle between dark and light mode. Your preferences are saved automatically.

3

Start Tracking

Search for any cryptocurrency using the search bar (or press Ctrl+K). Browse the market table, click any coin for AI analysis, or navigate to the Bitcoin Ecosystem, Precious Metals, or Stocks tabs.
